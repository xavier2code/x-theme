# When Text Becomes Art

In 90s print magazines, typography wasn't just communication—it was decoration. Headlines that bled off the page, drop caps that launched articles, and type treated like sculpture.

## The Serif Renaissance

**Playfair Display**, **Bodoni Moda**, **DM Serif Display**—these bold serif fonts carry the weight of editorial tradition.

### Scale Creates Hierarchy

- Display: 48-72px (headlines that demand attention)
- Body: 16-18px (comfortable reading)
- Caption: 12-14px (supporting context)

## The Tactile Web

We're moving away from the flat, sterile web of the 2010s. The new web has:
- Texture (paper-like backgrounds)
- Depth (hard shadows, overlapping elements)
- Personality (typography with character)

> "Good typography is invisible. Great typography is unforgettable."
