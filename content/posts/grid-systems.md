# Grid Systems in Web Design

A grid is more than lines on a page—it's the bones of visual communication.

## Why Grids Matter

Without a grid, content feels chaotic. With a grid, even chaos can feel intentional. The key is **asymmetry**—breaking the expected for effect.

### The Mathematics of Beauty

Consider the golden ratio (1:1.618). This proportion appears throughout nature, art, and... web design. An asymmetric 2:1 grid ratio creates visual tension that keeps readers engaged.

## Practical Applications

1. **Column Spans** - Let some content span 2 columns while other content takes just 1
2. **Baseline Grids** - Align text to a consistent rhythm
3. **Modular Grids** - Create repeatable units that organize complex information

> "Constraints breed creativity."

---

The grid is your friend. Learn it, then learn when to break it.
