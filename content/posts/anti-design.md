# Embracing the Unconventional

Sometimes the best design is the one that breaks all the rules.

## What is Anti-Design?

Anti-design rejects polish, symmetry, and visual harmony in favor of raw, attention-grabbing aesthetics. Think:
- Clashing colors
- Intentional misalignment
- Overwhelming visual density

## The Dribbble Effect

Platforms like Dribbble created pressure to produce *beautiful* work. But beautiful often means forgettable. Anti-design deliberately provokes—leaving lasting impressions.

### When to Break the Rules

Anti-design works best for:
- Fashion/Art publications
- Youth-oriented brands
- Portfolio pieces that need to stand out

> "Rules are made to be broken—especially design rules."

---

Balance is key. A little chaos makes order feel intentional.
